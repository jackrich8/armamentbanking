If you already have an Armament account and wish to register for Personal Internet Banking, you will be required to utilize your Social Security Number.


If you do not have a Social Security Number please contact us at 800.975.4722.


To gain immediate access to your Armament accounts, enter your Armament Card Number and PIN.


If you don’t have an Armament card or have forgotten your PIN, you will be able to continue the registration process and access Internet Banking once you receive your Electronic Banking Number (EBN) and 6 Digit Code.


If you have an Electronic Banking Number (EBN) and 6 Digit Code that were sent to you, select Verification Method as "EBN/6 Digit Code".

If you don't already have an Armament account, apply online. This will take less than 10 minutes to apply for an account for new customers, and less than 2 minutes for existing customers.
